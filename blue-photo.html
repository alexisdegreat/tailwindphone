About Our Company

Bowmenn is a digital tech-enabled logistics platform that connects Shippers with carriers to move freight.

We have combined our expertise in logistics with cargo owners and carriers to service an entire supply chain while keeping shippers in the driver’s seat.
With our experience in Logistics, our technology matches every load with the right truck, we have placed in your hands the power to manage your business with an upfront and unified price that is true to the market.

Bowmenn is currently accessible across Nigeria and is growing fast. Our proprietary technology includes mobile and web apps used for seamless delivery and moving experiences.
